this page is dedicated to the ongoing restoration of my grandfather's 1971 ford ranchero. this particular ranchero has been in my family since it was new, which also happened to be the year i was born. my grandfather was the first owner, and when he passed away it became my father's. while my father had the truck it got neglected and was left in his pasture for about 14 years. my father was a mechanic his whole life, and that is one skill i definitely didn't inherit. but when my father died i decided to take one of his projects and try to restore it.

originally i had intended to restore a 1947 studebaker pickup which my dad had bought and parked years before, but my mother suggested the ranchero as it would be less expensive to get running. once i truly thought about it, i realized that this truck has far more memories for me than the studebaker could ever have, and so the restoration began. i will document the repairs i make and take as many photos as possible so that everyone who is interested can follow along. i can only work on the truck in my spare time, so progress is rather slow going, but that's pretty much the same story with everyone i suppose. i'm not trying to bring it back to original, just to make it nice again because i don't plan on ever getting rid of it when it's finished.
